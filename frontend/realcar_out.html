FAQ

  • What is RealCar?
    RealCar is a premier luxury and exotic car rental service in South Beach, Miami, offering an exclusive selection of high-end vehicles. Our fleet includes Bentley rental in Miami and Porsche, alongside Rolls-Royce, Land Rover, BMW, and Cadillac. Our fleet features the latest models, available for both pickup and delivery to you in Miami, including Miami Beach and surrounding areas.
    Does RealCar offer exotic vehicles for rent?
    RealCar Miami offers an exclusive selection of exotic cars for rent, including some of the most sought-after models in the world. Rent a Porsche 911 Convertible for open-air freedom, a luxurious Rolls Royce Cullinan for ultimate opulence, or the Bentley Bentayga for an uncompromising fusion of performance and comfort.
    Does RealCar offer hourly rentals for exotic cars?
    RealCar does not rent exotic vehicles by the hour. The minimum billing period is for 24 hours, but you may return the vehicle earlier if you choose.
    What other automobile marques are available for rent with RealCar Miami?
    RealCar provides a wide selection of luxury and exotic vehicles for rent in Miami. From Land Rover rental in Miami to Porsche and Maserati, as well as Cadillac, Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Bentley, Rolls-Royce, and Lamborghini.
    Where is the RealCar office located in Miami?
    RealCar's office is located at 1000 5th Street, Miami Beach, FL 33139. If you plan to visit and complete your rental in person, please schedule an appointment in advance and specify the vehicle you’re interested in. This allows us to ensure it's ready for your arrival.
    Does RealCar deliver rental cars?
    Yes, we do! RealCar will deliver your rental vehicle to the location of your choice within Miami between 8 am and 8 pm. Deliveries outside of these hours can be arranged in advance. For an additional fee, you can also request delivery to any of the Miami-area airports, including Luxury car rental Miami International Airport (MIA), Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL), and Miami-Opa Locka Executive Airport (OPF). RealCar makes car rental delivery and return convenient for you.
    What are the requirements to rent with RealCar Miami?
    When renting a car with RealCar in Miami, you will need to provide your payment card information, driver's license number, and identity verification information. International drivers will also need to provide a valid passport and driver's license from their home country. We may require a security deposit of up to $3000, which will be refunded at the end of the rental. Please note that the payment card must be issued in the driver's name.
    Does RealCar require a security deposit?
    In most cases, no security deposit is required. If you are over 25 years old, have a valid driver’s license, and possess a major credit card in your name, such as AMEX or Chase Sapphire, a security deposit will not be necessary.
  • What is RealCar's cancellation policy for rental car reservations?
    RealCar's cancellation policy allows customers to cancel their reservation up to 72 hours before the start date for a full refund. Cancellations made within 72 hours will receive an e-credit for future rental reservations.
    What is the RealCar Miami fuel policy for luxury car rentals?
    All RealCar rental vehicles are delivered with a full tank of fuel and should be returned with the same fuel level. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel, the missing amount will be refilled at a rate of $6 per gallon.
    What is RealCar's smoking policy?
    RealCar maintains a strict no-smoking policy across its entire fleet. Renters and their passengers are prohibited from smoking in any RealCar rental vehicle. Reports of smoking are taken seriously, and if smoke is detected upon return of the vehicle, the renter may incur a fine of up to $250.
    What is RealCar's pet policy?
    RealCar Miami welcomes your pet along for the drive. However, if the rental vehicle's interior is returned excessively dirty, a cleaning fee of up to $250 may be charged.
    What is the best way to contact RealCar customer service?
    RealCar is always here to answer your questions or accommodate special travel needs. It's easy to manage your rental online with your RealCar account, but feel free to send a message via the Contact Us page, or call us at 305-306-2353 for further assistance.
    Does RealCar offer one-way car rentals?
    Yes. RealCar offers one-way car rentals for an additional fee. Pricing varies based on destination and vehicle type.
    To learn more, visit our dedicated One-Way Car Rental page.
    What is the process for returning a one-way rental?

    Yes, you can. RealCar allows one-way rentals from Miami, Florida to New York City, as well as other destinations, across our entire fleet.
    For pricing details and eligible routes, please visit our One-Way Car Rental page.
